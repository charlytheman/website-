Luxury Picnics



Your guide to an unforgettable experience



First, choose from one of our picnic packages.





Romantic Picnic Package.


A luxurious way to celebrate your partner or pop the question, a picnic that enchants with unforgettable romance. Perfect for Valentine’s Day, wedding anniversaries, date night or a weekend escape. Your picnic experience will be complete with card games, a fresh flower arrangement, and more! *Pricing starts $200 with accommodations for two people.




Group Picnic Package.


The perfect way to take ordinary moments and turn them into cherished memories. A picnic perfect for you + your loved ones, a group of friends, or a double date.This picnic experience is complete with card games, yard games (*where permitted), and more! *Pricing starts at $250 with accommodations for four people.




Picnic Celebration Package.


A fun way to take your get together to the next level. This is a celebratory picnic perfect for your birthday, bridal/baby shower, Mother’s/Father’s Day, or any occasion you’re wanting to celebrate. This experience will be complete with a small cake or specialty dessert! *Pricing starts at $350 with accommodations for six people. *Please note we require a two week advanced booking for picnic celebrations.
